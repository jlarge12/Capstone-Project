Welcome to the UK ATC Rewards Hub

The UK ATC Rewards Hub site provides an opportunity for all staff across TAG, Newcastle ATC, and Tech Regional North to engage in sustainable activity in exchange for compensation, rewards, and recognition. The aim of this site is to encourage you to make more environmentally conscious decisions and create sustainable habits not just around the office but in your every day life.
